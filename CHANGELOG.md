# Changelog

## 0.1.0 (2026-03-22)

Initial release.

- **ganit-core**: Vec2/Vec3/Vec4/Mat3/Mat4/Quat re-exports from glam, Transform2D, Transform3D, orthographic and perspective projections, lerp functions.
- **ganit-geo**: Ray, Plane, Aabb, Sphere primitives with ray-plane, ray-sphere (quadratic formula), and ray-AABB (slab method) intersection tests.
- **ganit-calc**: Central-difference derivative, trapezoidal and Simpson integration, linear interpolation, quadratic and cubic Bezier curves.
- **ganit-num**: Newton-Raphson and bisection root finding, Gaussian elimination with partial pivoting.
- **ganit-ai**: Daimon/hoosh client for AGNOS AI integration.
